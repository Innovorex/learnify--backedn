1. **Search on YouTube**: Use keywords like "IGNOU B.Ed BES-[course code]"

### Video Upload Guidelines for Future

When uploading videos manually:
1. Prefer IGNOU official videos when available
2. Use YouTube embed format: `https://www.youtube.com/embed/VIDEO_ID`
3. Ensure videos are educational and topic-relevant
4. Check that embedding is allowed (not all YouTube videos allow embedding)
5. Verify video plays correctly before saving

## Notes

- Some YouTube videos may show "Video unavailable" if embedding is disabled by uploader
- The platform now shows a "Video Coming Soon" placeholder for missing videos
- Users can still learn from comprehensive study notes even without videos
- For best results, source videos from official IGNOU channels when possible
